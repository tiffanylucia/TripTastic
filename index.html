Frequently
Asked Questions

Curious about how we help you travel better?
Just get your questions answered here.

What are your business opening hours? +
We are open Monday to Friday from 9:00 AM to 5:00 PM. On weekends, we are closed.
How do I make a booking? +
You can make a booking through our website or by calling our customer service number. Simply select your desired service and follow the prompts to complete your booking.
Do I need travel insurance? +
Yes, we highly recommend having travel insurance to cover any unexpected events such as trip cancellations, medical emergencies, or lost luggage. It provides peace of mind during your travels.
Do you offer discounts or promo codes? +
Yes, we frequently offer discounts and promo codes. You can find the latest offers on our website or subscribe to our newsletter to receive exclusive deals directly in your inbox.
Do you book airport transfers or shuttles? +
Yes, we can arrange airport transfers and shuttles for you. Please provide us with your flight details and we will organize the transportation to and from the airport.
Are meals included in the hotel booking? +
It depends on the hotel and the package you choose. Some hotels offer meal plans that include breakfast, lunch, and dinner, while others may only provide breakfast. Please check the details of your booking or contact us for clarification.
